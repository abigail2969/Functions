Golf Swing

Types of Golf Clubs

Driver

Used for long-distance shots from the tee. Has the largest clubhead.

Iron

Irons are used for various shots, usually from fairway to green.

Wedge

Wedges are designed for short, high-lofted shots around the green.

Putter

The putter is used on the green to roll the ball into the hole.

Golf Etiquette

  • Be quiet: Stay still and silent when others are hitting.
  • Maintain pace: Play at a reasonable speed and be ready for your shot.
  • Repair the course: Replace divots, rake bunkers, and fix ball marks on the green.
  • Safety first: Make sure no one is in range before swinging.
  • Respect the order: Wait your turn and let the player farthest from the hole hit first.
  • Dress properly: Follow the dress code of the golf course.
  • Be courteous: Congratulate good shots and respect fellow players.
